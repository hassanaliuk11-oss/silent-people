Silent Soul

Hassan Jatoi

Topic: Agony of Life
Life feels like an agony to me,
When your name smolders in my heart.
Though I may smile for the world,
Everything inside is falling apart.
You aren't here, yet every single moment,
Your absence dwells within my breath.
Love never taught me how to live,
It just made me accustomed to death.
I weave dreams with you in them,
But as I wake, they all scatter away.
Time heals nothing, it's just a lie they say.
You were a prayer that never came true,
And life now feels like a sentence to serve.

Topic: The Death Called Separation
Separation has become like death itself,
The breath flows, but the soul is gone.
Since you left, even time has frozen,
Days exist, but their meaning is withdrawn.
When I smile, my eyes begin to weep,
This laughter now holds no value anymore.
Your silence has slowly killed me,
Leaving no scream, no blame to restore.
I wanted you as much as I wanted life,
Now even life is no longer mine.
People tell me to have patience,
They don't know this pain is divine.
If you could return just one last time,
Perhaps this distance wouldn't be my end.
Topic: Nights Without Sleep
My nights pass without sleep,
Your memory stays awake inside my eyes.
Even the moon asks me the reason of my silence,
I smile and write only your name.
In quiet moments my heart talks to you,
Every heartbeat prays for your closeness.
Sleep has turned away from me,
Because my eyes have accepted you as destiny.
